About

Hi, we are Asjad and Aroob! A husband and wife photographer couple. We truly believe in the union of two hearts. Marriage and love, capturing the magical moments between two lovers. Working with different people and cultures makes us happy. Our moto is to build a friendly and comfortable relation with the people because this is when they fully express what they feel.

Asjad Azeem

Photographer, Cinematographer

I have always been interested in capturing emotions, may it be in any form. Weddings bring more joy to me personally because everyone is so happy! I enjoy landscape and street photography as well.
